ABOUT THE CENTER

Cat help center "REMI" was registered on February 12, 2021. Our main goals are:
1. Providing necessary assistance to stray and distressed animals.
2. Providing animals with temporary home, food and veterinary assistance to look for permanent homes.
3. Control of the stray animal population by implementing the "catch-sterilize-release" program.
4. Promoting public training on animal care, health, protection and welfare.
5. Raising funds from natural and legal persons as donations or gifts for the maintenance and treatment of animals.

Some meet each other for life and become friends and family, others to come into each other's lives for only a short time, to teach and remind us of how fragile life is, the importance of time, the importance of compassion and ability to see what's going on around.
